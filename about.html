About Us

At Endangered Threads we are passionate about preserving the memory, raising awareness, and increasing conservation efforts of our planet's endangered species through trendy clothing. When you wear our clothing, you are spreading awareness through fashion vibrantly and stylishly. There are not enough clothing companies that spread awareness through their products. At Endangered Threads, we are doing just that: using our products to raise awareness about endangered species. Our profits will be used to hold fundraisers and donate to organizations that make it their mission to conserve endangered animals and their habitats.

About the Founder

Founded by an avid wildlife enthusiast and conservation advocate, Endangered Threads was born out of a passion for both fashion and environmental sustainability. This high school student saw an opportunity to blend these interests by creating a brand that not only offers stylish clothing but also contributes to the preservation of endangered species. She is committed to making a positive impact on the world through responsible business practices and community engagement.
