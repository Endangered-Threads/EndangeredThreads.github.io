Frequently Asked Questions

Question Answer
What is the purpose of Endangered Threads? The purpose of Endangered Threads is to preserve the memory, raise conservation awareness, and increase conservation efforts of our planet's endangered species through trendy clothing. When you wear our clothing, you are spreading awareness through fashion vibrantly and stylishly.
How does my purchase help endangered species? Proceeds will be donated to wildlife conservation organizations working to protect endangered species.
How can I learn more about endangered animals? Our website has a section on endangered animals where you can learn more about them, current wildlife conservation efforts, different ways you can get involved, and how you can become a wildlife ambassador.
Who should I contact if I have questions about the organization? If you have any questions, feel free to email info@endangered-threads.net. We will get back to you as soon as possible.

Terminology

Term Definition
Endangered Animals Animals that are at risk of extinction due to habitat loss, poaching, climate change, or other factors.
Wildlife Conservation Efforts and actions taken to protect and preserve wildlife species and their habitats.
Wildlife Ambassador A person who promotes different organizations whose mission is to protect endangered species.
Conservation Awareness Educating and informing the public about the importance of protecting endangered species and their habitats.
