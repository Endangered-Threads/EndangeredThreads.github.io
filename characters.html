Our Characters

Koko the Koala

Koko is our beloved koala who lives in Australia and represents the vulnerable koala population. Her mission is to spread awareness about habitat loss and the need for conservation.

Blaze the Tiger

Blaze is Endangered Thread's fearless tiger who lives in Asia and symbolizes the endangered tigers. His goal is to educate people about the dangers of poaching and deforestation.
